**FLUENS v1.0 ‚Äî The Engine of Liquid Pedagogy**  

FLUENS does not deliver information ‚Äî it tunes it.  
Its operational core, the **Cognitive Tuning Engine (MAC)**, is a modular structure that adjusts every interaction according to the user‚Äôs emergent signals.  

### 1. Cognitive Sensory Input (CSI)
